Achievements

18 April,2020

Get VISA Award

Pseudo Bank has achieved the "Excellence in Product Innovation" and "Excellence in Prepaid Card Business" awards conferred by VISA, a global payment service provider. These two awards are given in recognition of instant virtual VISA card generation.

20 July,2020

Crosses One Million Taka Deposit Milestone

Pseudo Bank is now a bank with a deposit of one Trillion Taka. The country's top private commercial bank crossed the milestone on July 20, 2020. In 2019, the bank's deposit was Tk 94681 crore, in 2018 it was Tk 82257 crore.

2 August,2020

"Best CEO Award"

Farhana Meem, Managing Director and CEO of Pseudo Bank achieved ‘Best Emerging CEO in Banking 2020’ award conferred by UK-based financial intelligence house Cambridge IF Analytica. She received the award from Shahid Malik, Former British Minister .

9 september,2020

"Best Corporate Bank"

Pseudo Bank secured the First Position as the Best Corporate in the Banking Sector of ICMAB Best Corporate Award-2020 conferred by The Institute of Cost and Management .Finance Minister Abul Maal Abdul Muhith, MP handed over the award to Farhana Meem.
